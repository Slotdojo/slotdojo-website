THE KATA

Three sections. Every machine.

A SlotDojo scenario isn't a tip and it isn't a prediction. It's a disciplined kata: a repeatable form of practice for one situation on one machine. Every scenario has exactly three sections in the same order, every time.

01

What to Look For

The walk-up criteria. Meter values, visible state, bonus indicators, denom, neighbor traffic. The signals on the cabinet that tell you this machine, right now, deserves your seat.

EXAMPLE Buffalo Gold with 4 or more gold heads visible on the trigger column.
02

All the Details

The mechanics. The math. How the bonus actually triggers. Why volatility shapes the wait. Bet structure. The reason this scenario carries positive expected value, in plain language.

EXAMPLE Why max-bet on Lightning Link only matters for the Grand. The break-even cost of getting there.
03

Stop Playing

The exit criteria. The signals that the favorable window has closed. The discipline of leaving with what you came for, not feeding it back chasing the next spin.

EXAMPLE Hit the minor. Pocket it. The meter resets and the next 90 minutes is a different game.

Not every scenario applies to every player. Volatility, bankroll, and floor traffic all shape which seats are yours. The dojo doesn't tell you what to do. It teaches you what to recognize.
